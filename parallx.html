KAIVAL GYAAN SAMPRADYA

PARAMGURU SHREEMAT KARUNASAGAR is Known by several identities. He is known as a "Kaival Vetta" (meaning : He knows everything about kaival karta). He is the Param Vishesh Ansh of kaival karta. kaival karta is THE one and only universal GOD. Karta has no gender, Name, Form, Figure, or shape. Karta sent paramguru to give everyone GYAN (knowledge) about kaival karta. There is only one GOD (kaival Karta) and nobody can compare to Karta.

He was initiated as a saint on Jeth Sood Poonam, Vikram Samvat 2026 at Banaras by the sixth Kuveracharya H.H. Shitaldasji Mahrajshri.
